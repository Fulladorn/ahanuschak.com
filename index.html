About Me

My name is Aaron Hanuschak and I'm currently a 3rd year Computer Science student at the University of Florida. A few of my hobbies include Technology, Cyber Security and Hockey.

I created this web page to serve as a home base for all things homelab. My homelab consists of a cluster of retired server equipment that I use to learn more about enterprise infrastructure, security and development. This home page is just a static landing page however many of the other webpages linked at the top will lead to other things I am currently working on.

I've recently taken a strong interest in cyber security and a lot of the projects I work on in my homelab have a heavy emphasis on security. Feel free to take a look at my blog to learn more about the various projects I've completed and currently working on.
